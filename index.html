Rollercoaster Modeler

Controls:

  • Left-drag: Look around (mouse / trackpad)
  • Middle-drag: Pan camera
  • Mouse Wheel: Zoom
  • WASD: Move forward/left/back/right
  • Q/E or Ctrl/Space: Move down/up (Ctrl = Up, Space = Down)
  • 1-4: Snap to viewpoint (Front, Top, Side, Isometric)
  • V: Add vertex at random position
  • Del / Backspace: Delete selected vertex
  • IJKL: Move selected vertex (Ctrl/Cmd for Z-axis)
  • Shift+Arrows: Move faster
  • R: Reset scene

Click vertices to select them. Edit coordinates in the HUD panel on the left.
